Make your token tradeable.

Pair your token with the network's coin to create a trading pool. OpenHelix prepares everything and hands you off to the official, audited exchange to confirm — your funds only ever move on the DEX you already trust.

How it works

Open the exchange

The button takes you to PancakeSwap (BNB), Uniswap (other EVM), or Raydium (Solana) with your token pre-loaded.

Choose the amounts

Decide how many of your tokens and how much of the network coin (BNB/ETH/SOL) to put in. This sets the starting price.

Approve & confirm

Approve the token, then confirm "Add Liquidity" in your wallet. You receive LP tokens representing your share of the pool.

Lock your LP (recommended)

Lock those LP tokens so buyers know the pool can't be pulled. Use the Lock tab in Studio (EVM testnet for now).

OpenHelix's own locking & vesting go live on mainnet once audited. Today you can lock LP on a test network, or use a reputable third-party locker on mainnet.

Before you add real liquidity

Whatever you pair becomes the starting price and is exposed to traders. Start small, lock your LP, and never add more than you can afford to lose. None of this is financial advice.
